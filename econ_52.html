Econ 52: Economic Analysis III
(Intermediate Macroeconomics)

Econ 52 is the third course in the Economics Core requirement for the undergraduate major. It was taught by Adrien Auclert and Pete Klenow in Winter 2021, and by Patrick Kehoe in Spring 2021 and Spring 2022. It is about both long-run growth and short-run business cycles. The course focuses on the effects of fiscal policy (spending, taxes, and debt) and monetary policy. It makes extensive use of calculus.
